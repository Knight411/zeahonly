Rules & Exceptions

1. Stay on Zeah as much as possible!
2. If you die and spawn in Lumbridge, you must run/teleport back to Zeah.
3. Druidic Ritual is allowed, but all 4 items must be obtained on Zeah.
4. Buying a pestle and mortar is optional.
5. Leaving for a barbarian fishing rod is optional.
6. Completing Eagle's Peak is optional, but not recommended once Aerial Fishing is released.
7. Completing Lost City is optional.
8. Holiday events are optional.

Why can't I play however I want to?

You absolutely can!

This is simply a rough guide and you are in no way obligated to follow it.
However, the line must be drawn somewhere, otherwise any account could be included on the HiScores.

If you decide to play with different rules, no worries! You'll still be included in a different category on the HiScores page.
